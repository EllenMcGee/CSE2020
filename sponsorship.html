Why Sponsor CSE 2020?

The second Conference on Sustainability in Engineering will be held at the University of Waterloo in February 2020, and we are seeking sponsors to make this conference a resounding success and to promote the importance of sustainability as we move into the future.

The conference will be an excellent opportunity for future engineering leaders to learn about the many aspects of sustainability and its increasing importance in our profession which is perfectly reflected in this year’s theme ‘Planting Seeds for Sustainability.’ Over 150 students from 44 Canadian academic institutions will participate in events revolving around learning, networking, and meaningful discussion in order to understand the importance of sustainability.

As a sponsor of this conference, you will have a unique opportunity to interact with engineering students, and potential future employees, from across Canada who are committed to making a positive difference in our society. Your support will help us host a variety of events, as well as give you the opportunity to visit our state of the art campus at the University of Waterloo, interact with our successful student led sustainability initiatives, and promote your company to delegates from the top Canadian universities.

Please see the sponsorship package for full details of the conference and the options for support. We hope you will consider supporting this unique opportunity. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to the sponsorship team at sponsorship@cse.cfes.ca
